Conceptual model

The editorial-engineering panel

Tracked uppercase mono label above a serif title — the signature header pattern, on a quiet nested surface.

Panels are the primary container: white (or surface-2) fill, 1px hairline border, 8px radius, flat by default. Structure comes from hairlines and white space, not shadow.
