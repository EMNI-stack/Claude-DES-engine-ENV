Model & Floor

Place resources and storage on the floor and connect them into a route. Travel time comes from the distance between placed nodes, so where things sit changes how the line performs. Move a node, re-run, and watch the cycle time change.

Early build: a single linear route. Choose how loads move — instant delay, a capacity-limited conveyor, or a shared worker pool. Branching and assembly come next.

Pick a tool, then click the canvas to place a node. In Move, drag a node to reposition it; click to select and edit. The route follows the order nodes are added (edit it on the right).
